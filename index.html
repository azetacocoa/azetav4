Our Story

Where Science Meets the Soil

Azeta is a tree-to-bar chocolate brand born in the serene cacao growing region of Anamalai, Tamil Nadu. Founded by Vibhu Nishanth, a chemical engineer turned chocolate craftsman, Azeta is where scientific precision meets agricultural heritage.

For many years the cacao cultivated from our family owned farm was sold to commercial brands in India. Today, with in house grinding, fermentation and world class machinery, we have created Azeta, making a myriad of quality cacao products, while being ethical and hygienic.

By combining time honoured practices with modern process control, Azeta delivers chocolate that balances depth, consistency, and character.

Our Farm

The Home of Our Cacao Trees

Nestled in the forest areas near the Anamalai Tiger Reserve lies our cacao farm. This farm is not just home to Theobroma Cacao,the cacao tree but also hosts a multitude of other cultivation crops including Coconut, Areca nut, Nutmeg and Pepper.

At our farm, we believe in a sustainable farming system where we don't disturb the ecosystem, wildlife, or use any pesticides. Water is managed through controlled drip irrigation, and harvesting is done entirely by hand.

No Pesticides

Our farming follows pesticide free practices, working with the land rather than against it.

Multi Crop Farming

Coconut, Areca nut, Nutmeg and Pepper grow alongside cacao, promoting natural biodiversity.

Hand Harvesting

Every pod is manually harvested at peak ripeness and processed on site the same day.

Drip Irrigation

Water efficient irrigation keeps the soil healthy without stressing surrounding ecosystems.

Anamalai Tiger Reserve

Growing in the Shadow of the Wild

Our cacao grows near one of India's most protected forest reserves. We farm in a way that respects the wildlife corridor, preserves soil integrity, and avoids chemical intervention because the health of the land is the foundation of every bar we make.
